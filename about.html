Our story

From small residential gates to full warehouse roofing, Gabakssteel has grown through referrals by delivering consistent quality and standing by our work.

We operate from Mafoluku, Oshodi — serving Lagos and nearby states. Our team handles design, cutting, welding, surface finishing and on‑site installation with safety compliance.

  • Powder‑coat options
  • Hot‑dip galvanizing
  • Anti‑corrosion prep
  • After‑sales support

Timeline

  1. 2010 — Company founded; first estate gate contracts in Oshodi/Isolo.
  2. 2014 — Added street‑light pole fabrication and municipal installs.
  3. 2018 — Expanded to overhead tank towers and warehouse roofing.
  4. 2023 — Laser‑cut patterns and stainless railing offerings.
  5. Today — Full‑service steel fabrication for homes and industry across Lagos.
